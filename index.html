Skills & Expertise

UX Technique & Methods

UX extensive research

UXR involves comprehensive studies of user behaviors, needs, and experiences through various methods like surveys, interviews, data analysis, usability testing etc. to inform and enhance design decisions.

Critical Thinking

It requires logical reasoning, open-mindedness, and the ability to identify biases and gaps in knowledge which helps in analyzing, evaluating, and synthesizing information objectively to form reasoned judgments and solve problems effectively.

Ethnographic Study

It involves immersive observation and interaction to understand people's cultures and behaviors. It makes product simple, clear, and easy to use.

Competitive analysis

Identifying and evaluating competitors to understand their strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and market positioning. This helps in making informed decisions and gaining a competitive advantage

Persona Development

Persona development creates detailed user profiles based on research, guiding design decisions with empathy and understanding of user needs, behaviors, and goals.

Information Architecture

IA structures content to enhance ux by ensuring information is accessible, understandable, and navigable within digital interfaces. It focuses on usability and findability, facilitating efficient interaction and comprehension.

Wireframe /Prototype

Wireframes are skeletal outlines or blueprints that represent the framework of a website or application, focusing on layout and functionality, whether, Prototypes are interactive representations of a design concept, allowing for testing and refinement before final development.

Constructive communication

It involves exchanging ideas and information in a respectful and productive manner. It focuses on clarity, active listening, empathy, and finding solutions collaboratively.

Leadership / Collaboration

Leadership involves guiding and inspiring a team, peers towards a shared vision, while collaboration is working together towards common goals, leveraging diverse skills and perspectives for collective success.

Cognitive Empathy

The ability to recognize and understand someone else's emotions, thoughts, and perspectives, allowing for a deeper insight into their experiences.

SWOT analysis

It empowers organizations by highlighting strengths to leverage, weaknesses to improve, opportunities to seize, and threats to mitigate, fostering strategic foresight and competitive advantage

Data Visualization

Graphical representation of information and data. It transforms complex datasets into clear, accessible visuals such as charts, graphs, and maps to facilitate understanding, analysis, and decision-making.

Usability heuristics

Usability heuristics are guidelines or principles used to evaluate user interfaces for usability issues based on established best practices.

User testing

User testing involves observing and gathering feedback from users interacting with a product to identify usability problems and areas for improvement.

Design Documentation

A collection of taxonomical metadata that classifies the understanding of development specifications in design system.
